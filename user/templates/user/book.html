About Chinadan

Born from a collective of distillers and collectors, Chinadan pursues terroir‑driven spirits with uncompromising standards, from grain selection to barrel finish. Every release is authenticated, documented, and cellared for profile maturity.

Our Pillars

Everything we bottle upholds four non‑negotiables that define quality, longevity, and truth in spirit.

Provenance

Source transparency from field to barrel with audited chain‑of‑custody records.

Craft

Traditional distillation, slow cuts, and patient maturation to preserve character.

Integrity

No flavoring, no shortcuts — only honest aging, blending, and finishing.

Stewardship

Sustainable sourcing, responsible allocations, and archival care for future palates.
